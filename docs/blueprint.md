# **App Name**: ArtisanLane

## Core Features:

- Product Catalog: Display a curated collection of artisanal coffee products.
- Subscription Frequency: Enable users to customize their subscription frequency (weekly, bi-weekly, monthly).
- Taste Profile Quiz: AI tool providing personalized coffee recommendations based on a user's taste profile, enhancing discovery and satisfaction.
- Account Management: Allow registered users to manage their subscriptions, addresses, payment methods and preferences.
- Payment Gateway: Secure payment processing integration.
- Informational Pages: Display and allow interaction with informational and static content.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Rich, deep brown (#A67B5B) to evoke the aroma and warmth of coffee.
- Background color: Soft beige (#F5F5DC), providing a neutral and comforting backdrop that emphasizes the product images and text.
- Accent color: Muted gold (#D4A373), used sparingly to highlight key interactive elements.
- Body and headline font: 'Literata' serif, creating a literary and slightly formal feel that emphasizes quality and heritage.
- Simple, elegant line icons in the muted gold accent color.
- Clean and minimalist layout with ample whitespace to showcase the coffee products.
- Subtle transitions and loading animations to enhance the user experience without being distracting.
